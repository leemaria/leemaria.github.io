Psi Installs and Senior Roast

It is with a cross between a sad an happy heart that we send off our seniors ever year. Sad because we will not be able to see these sisters every day, as we have for the past four years; but happy because we know that they are entering a new stage of life with new adventures and challenges. To celebrate this momentous occasion, we hold a banquet every year to commemorate our graduating seniors, and share pictures and stories that empitomize their four years as a sister of alpha Kappa Delta Phi.

It is also at this time that we welcome our new class of sisters into our eternal sisterhood. Also, we celebrate the accomplishments of individual sisters, as well as the house as a whole, for the previous school year.
