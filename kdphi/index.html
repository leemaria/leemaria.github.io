Welcome

You have reached the website of the University of Michigan, Lambda Chapter of the alpha Kappa Delta Phi Sorority, Incorporated. We are the fastest-growing and the largest Asian-interest sorority nationwide. We were established in the 1990 at the University of California, Berkeley, and and have since then grown to over 46 chapters from the East coast to the West coast.

You can find almost everything you need to know about our chapter on this website. If you are considering of rushing alpha Kappa Delta Phi, this is one of the great ways to know how our organization is formed and how active we are in the university and its community.

Go Blue, THINK PINK

The month of October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month. **Every 13 minutes, a woman dies from breast cancer. 1 of 8 women will be diagnosed with breast cancer in their life. 40,000 women will die of breast cancer this year.** Help us spread awareness about this life-threatening disease by attending our events, or donating to our events. 100% of all proceeds that we make go to the Avon Breast Cancer Crusade for breast cancer research, awareness, and support. Please click here to view our upcoming Breast Cancer related events! Or come out to the Diag on Thursday, October 21 from 10am to 3pm to decorate BOOB COOKIES. Grab some lunch on Friday, October 22 in the Psych Atrium of East Hall at our Think Pink Food Festival (Contact any sister for tickets). Or party it up for the cause at Necto Night Club on Saturday, October 23! (Again, contact any sister for tickets!)
