Fusion

Client: alpha Kappa Delta Phi Sorority and Lambda Phi Epsilon Fraternity

Date: January 21st, 2011

Description: Flyer designed for Lunar Ball 2011, an event that celebrates Lunar New Years. Red and yellow are both festive colors representing luck, fortune and prosperity in Chinese culture. Thus, I wanted to create a flyer that was eye catching as well as relevent to fellow Chinese students on the University of Michigan campus. 2011 is the year of the rabbit in the Chinese zodiac.
