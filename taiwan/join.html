Membership

Joining TWSA is very easy! Just check out our schedule and come to one of our events, or you can email us at twsa-feedback@umich.edu. Your emails will be answered!

You can also find us on facebook at http://www.facebook.com/home.php#/group.php?gid=5581717655.

New Students

Interested in joining a club that showcases Taiwanese culture, promotes cross-student organization relatinships, and assists Taiwanese international students, all in a friendly environment? Then definitely check out TWSA! We hold many events throughout the year, including our annual Welcome BBQ (beginning of fall semester), Clubbing Night, Chinese New Year, and our infamous Ski Trip (during Winter Semester)! Be sure to check out our schedule for our other events (such as Halloween Party, Meet & Greet the Board, etc).
If you have any questions, or would like more information, email us at twsa-feedback@umich.edu!

International Students

Never moved to a different country before? You have nothing to worry about! TWSA encourages incoming international taiwanese students to contact us with their arrival information. We have several of our board members drive to the airport to give rides (free!) every year.
